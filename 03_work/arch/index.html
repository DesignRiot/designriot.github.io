Arch
Design management, 3D modelling + visualisation.

We have a passion for architecture and design quality with a focus on two key specialisms; Design Management and 3D Modelling.

As design managers, we work to oversee all design activity in architectural offices to help enable collaborative working environments and ensure a consistent, coordinated approach to each project in a designer’s portfolio.

As 3D modellers, we take pride in producing all our models and images in-house, so that you can be assured of the highest quality service that a given timeframe allows. All our projects are planned and programmed using our design management procedures, so you know exactly what to expect and when.

"Our commitment to design quality and spaces that work is matched by our design integrity, ethics and attention to detail."

DESIGN MANAGEMENT

DESIGN PROGRAMMING

When prepared well, design programmes are the best way to reflect the design process and key dates. We work collaboratively with the Design Team to provide a service that puts the design process first.

OBJECTIVES + STRATEGY

We work to capture, justify and communicate project objectives to all those who need to be involved.

We have the experience to facilitate a process that helps ensure prevent unnecessary disruption later in the design process.

STRUCTURE + PROCESSES

3D MODELLING + VISUALISATION

CLAY CONCEPTS

The clay pack is a key part of the 3D Design Development process allowing designers and the client to really explore massing, architectural form and space before delving into details and materiality.

3D MODELLING

3D architectural models offer an unparalleled immersive experience of a building to both you as a designer and your client.
