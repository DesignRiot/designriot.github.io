Background

Ivana is a UK registered architect with experience in various sectors within the construction industry. She holds a BA (Hons) with first-class honours and a MArch in Architecture with distinction from the Manchester School of Architecture and an Advanced Diploma in Professional Practice from the Royal Institute of British Architects.


Ivana is a driven innovator whose research aims to explore the huge potential of data to develop dynamic digital models and tools for the analysis of complex behaviours. She has worked on numerous research projects including the curation, and contribution towards, an exhibition on Japan's changing urban culture in collaboration with Hosei University which was showcased in Manchester and Tokyo.


Ivana currently provides architectural consulting services specialising in BIM and 3D modelling whilst undertaking funded research with the Warwick Institute for the Science of Cities. Ivana’s research aim is to explore the huge potential of data to develop dynamic digital models and tools for the analysis of complex behaviours. Ivana hopes to open the possibility for the design of truly ‘intelligent’ buildings to help in the creation of new, user-centred spaces that respond to demand and needs in real time.
