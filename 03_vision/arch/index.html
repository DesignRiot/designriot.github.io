We have a passion for architecture and design quality with a focus on two key specialisms; Design Management and 3D Modelling.

As design managers, we work to oversee all design activity in architectural offices to help enable collaborative working environments and ensure a consistent, coordinated approach to each project in a designer’s portfolio.

BIM + 3D MODELLING

3D architectural models offer an unparalleled immersive experience of a building to both you as a designer and your client to help you walk them through their project in vivid and highly life-like detail.

Visualisations allow for effective, efficient design communication helping Clients overcome challenges of technical understanding.

Planning applications featuring 3D design models significantly increase the successful passage because they allow them to quickly and accurately grasp the dimensions, materials, design and a response to context.

"Our client’s success is crucial to us so working with Drees opens access to a talented pool of specialised in house and associate services."

The clay packs are a key part of the 3D Design Development process allowing designers and the client to really explore massing, architectural form and space before delving into details and materiality.
