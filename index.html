Drees Consulting Ltd is a UK based creative design and management studio providing both architectural design and professional research services.

RESEARCH

We specialise in architectural research with a focus on the socio-spatial relationships between the built environment and human behaviour. We aim to help develop our clients' sector expertise to enhances credibility and provides competitive advantage.

As professional researchers, we endeavour to contribute original, empirical knowledge by helping companies undertake rigorous experiments, gathering data and analysing results to help drive innovative architectural solutions and validate concepts.

A key aspect of our scope, we are responsible for the project management and cross-disciplinary collaboration between stakeholders. Once published we also provide all the outreach – including conferences and speaking engagements – to promote the research funded by your organisation.

ARCHITECTURE

We are trained designers and problem solvers able to bring real value to your architectural project.

We pride ourselves on our ability to understand intricate design briefs and produce outstanding architectural services; exceeding client expectations and delivering beyond the core requirements.

We are a friendly, open-minded team of two whose work is inspired by people, culture and space. Whatever the project – big or small, bold or sensitive – we answer your brief without ever losing sight of your practical, budgetary or commercial needs or wants
